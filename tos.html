Terms of Service

Last updated 12/28/2024.

If you're interested in a commission, please contact me on Bluesky first, not Discord (I don't accept unexpected friend requests)!

After discussing the details of your request, we can move to Discord!



General Terms

  • I have the right to decline or cancel a commission at any stage. If canceled after payment, you will receive a refund.
  • I require full payment (via PayPal invoice, USD only) after you accept a final sketch from me.
  • Additional characters are +100% of the original price, excluding shading.
  • You must credit me when you post/use work commissioned from me. (You can link to my website, Twitter, or Bluesky.)
  • By contacting me regarding commissions, you agree to the entirety of the Terms of Service.

I accept:

  • SFW Commissions
  • Commissions with visual references
  • Personal use commissions (setting as PFP, banner, etc)
  • Human/furry/animal characters, and real people (on a case-by-case basis, since I'm not confident with this yet)

I don't accept:

  • NSFW or suggestive commissions
  • Text-only descriptions in place of character references
  • Commercial use of my work (ex. selling on a T-shirt)
  • Commissions with machines/vehicles/guns, gore, or hateful imagery
  • My work being used to train AI or sold as NFTs
  • AI generated images/text used as references


Edit Policy

Minor edits: Small changes like color edits, adding/editing text, etc.
Intermediate edits: Adding small elements (e.g. background items), changing proportions
Major edits: Adding large elements (e.g. another character), changing character pose

Minor and intermediate edits are free during the sketch process. Afterwards, in the line-art, coloring and shading stages, there will be a fee.

  • Minor edits: 5 minor edits are free before a $5 fee for each set of 5 minor edits.
  • Intermediate edits: After sketch has been approved, +$10-$20 depending on extent of changes, and whether line-art has been started or not. Not allowed after coloring stage has been started.
  • Major edits: Not allowed after sketch has been approved.

Payment is required upfront for edits.
