About Me

Hi! My name's Quinn. I'm most confident in my ability to draw anthropomorphic characters, but I'm hoping to widen my comfort zone by drawing different characters of varying species. That's why I take commissions!

My goal as an artist is to one day be able to paint vibrant splash arts - like the artists at Riot Games!
